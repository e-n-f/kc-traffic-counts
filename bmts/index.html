Traffic Counts

BMTS traffic counts for road segments within the BMTS planning area.

Each year BMTS hires summer employees to update turning movement counts at signalized intersections.  Usually the counts are updated on a three-year cycle and only the most recent counts are posted online.  For historical counts or if you do not see the intersection you are looking for, please call our office at 607-778-2443.  We add new counts each summer.
